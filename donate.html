Donation Guidelines

We Accept:

  • Unopened, non-expired canned goods
  • Dry goods (pasta, rice, cereal)
  • Unopened packaged foods
  • Fresh produce (in good condition)
  • Unopened baby food and formula
  • Commercially packaged baked goods

We Cannot Accept:

  • Homemade or prepared foods
  • Expired or damaged items
  • Opened packages
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Medications or supplements
  • Perishable items requiring refrigeration (unless coordinated in advance)

Quick & Easy Process

Our volunteers will arrive at your scheduled time, so you don't need to wait around. Just leave your donation in a visible spot if you won't be home.

Safe & Verified

All our volunteers undergo background checks and follow strict safety protocols to ensure a secure donation process.
