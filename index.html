How NeighborShare Works

A simple three-step process to connect food donors with those in need through our dedicated volunteers

1. Schedule Pickup

Residents schedule a pickup time through our simple online form or mobile app.

2. Volunteer Collection

Our verified volunteers collect donations directly from your doorstep at the scheduled time.

3. Safe Delivery

Food is safely transported to local food banks and distributed to families in need.
